Rationale

This webpage is meant to celebrate african artists as well as highlighting issues with in the art community on a local and global scale. It will feature up and coming artists to help promote them as well as giving them a platform to share their views and ideas with the world and just through art. The main focus of the data visualization is to bring awareness to the stolen African art cause. I feel as though it is important to return the art to their rightfull owners. The data emphasizes jsut how much art has been taken and the amount of African countries impacted.
