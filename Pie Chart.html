Pie chart how much of Sub-Saharan Africa's art is present in Europe.

Data Analysis

There are only two values used in this Pie chart. One is largely greater than the other. I used a pie chart to emphasize the size difference between the two values by showing how much space one takes up compared to the other. The pie chart is easy to read as it draws the viewer's attention to the larger item on the chart, which is what is needed to show the viewer how pressing the issue is.
