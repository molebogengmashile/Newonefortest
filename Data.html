Bar graph representing the amount of African artifacts at Musée du quai Branly, Paris

Data Analysis

The bar graph represents the number of African artifacts that are kept at the Musée du quai Branly museum in Paris. I chose a bar graph because it only shows the number of countries this museum is keeping art from but also how much. The bar graph allows the viewer to focus on one bar at a time and draw as much information as possible while being able to make comparisons to the other bars on the graph. Data from here
