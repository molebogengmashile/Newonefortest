African Art




This webpage is meant to celebrate African artists as well as highlighting issues with in the art community on a local and global scale. It will feature up and coming artists to help promote them as well as giving them a platform to share their views and ideas with the world and just through art. There is also a large focus on African art history as it is important that we acknowledge and appreciate the history of African art and how it impacts African artists today.
